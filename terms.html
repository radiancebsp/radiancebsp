Refund Rules

1. General

  • Form amount / kit amount will not be refunded.
  • No refund of registration fee shall be made under any circumstances whatsoever.

2. Short-Term Course

  • No fee refund of any type will be made under any circumstances after joining the short-term classroom/online courses.

3. Online Live Course

  • Full refund can be claimed within 15 days from the start of the batch, apart from the kit amount.
  • After 15 days, no refunds will be processed under any circumstances.
  • Kit amount for the Online Live Program is non-refundable.

4. Offline Classroom Course

  • 15 days: 20% of the tuition fee without scholarship will be deducted.
  • 30 days: 40% of the tuition fee without scholarship will be deducted.
  • 45 days: 50% of the tuition fee without scholarship will be deducted.
  • 60 days: 60% of the tuition fee without scholarship will be deducted.
  • 90 days: 70% of the tuition fee without scholarship will be deducted.
  • 120 days: 80% of the tuition fee without scholarship will be deducted.
  • The kit amount is non-refundable.
  • Refund requests will not be accepted after 120 days.
Important: Any legal matter or dispute related to fee refund is subject to Bilaspur (Chhattisgarh) jurisdiction only. Radiance handles refund cases through email only; no phone calls will be entertained for the refund process.
