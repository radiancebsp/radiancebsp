Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common doubts parents and students ask us.

How are classes conducted? In-person or online?

We offer both in-person and online classes to cater to the diverse needs of our students. Our state-of-the-art classrooms provide a conducive learning environment, and our online platform ensures flexibility for those who prefer to study from the comfort of their homes.

What makes Radiance Coaching Classes different from other coaching centers?

Radiance stands out for its holistic approach to education. We prioritize not only academic excellence but also the overall development of our students. Our innovative teaching methodologies, Conceptual learning, personalized attention, and a focus on building strong foundations set us apart.

How does Radiance Coaching Classes support students beyond regular classes?

We believe in providing comprehensive support to our students. Apart from regular classes, we offer doubt-solving sessions, mock exams, and one-on-one counselling. Our goal is to guide students through their academic journey and help them overcome challenges.

Are there scholarship programs available?

: Yes, Radiance Coaching Classes is committed to making quality education accessible. We have scholarship programs based on merit and financial need. Details about eligibility criteria and application processes can be found on our website or by contacting our admissions team.

Can I visit the coaching centre before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students and their parents to visit our coaching centre, meet our faculty, and get a feel for the learning environment. You can schedule a visit by contacting our front desk.

How does Radiance Coaching Classes ensure a safe learning environment, especially in the current scenario?

The safety and well-being of our students are our top priorities. We strictly adhere to all health and safety guidelines. In addition to in-person classes, we offer online options to ensure that students can continue their education safely from home if needed.
