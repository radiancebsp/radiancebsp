About Us

(click on the image)

Radiance Coaching Classes is presented as a student-centered institute built on strong teaching, personal mentorship, and a belief that every student deserves a fair chance to succeed. Its mission focuses on inspiring confidence, resilience, and growth through guided learning, while its vision is to make quality education accessible and supportive, without letting financial barriers block ambition.

The history shows steady growth from its beginnings in 2013 to a wider academic reach, adoption of online learning during 2020, and continued success in recent years. The overall identity of Radiance is that of a caring learning community where students are not only prepared for exams, but also encouraged to grow into capable, thoughtful, and confident individuals.

Our mission: Make high-quality, career-focused education accessible and engaging.
